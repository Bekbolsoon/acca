Acca

ACCA is the first Computer Art Auction House in WEB3. In 2021, the world of computer art was revived with the invention of NFT technology, which enables the identification of a digital artwork's author and sales history. This breakthrough and the advent of blockchain technologies gave rise to new types of WEB3 art projects, such as - NFT art marketplaces. As a result, a vibrant new cultural layer has emerged, consisting of new underground collectors, young digital artists, and new curators of computer art.

After the first waive of computer art renaissance in 2021, the popularity of NFTs a downturn alongside a natural curation process that sifted out projects deemed irrelevant. In our opinion, the next stage in the evolution of the art industry in WEB3 is the traditional art institutions' stage of transformation.

It's time to place the auction industry's future in the community's hands and fully trust their intuition to reshape the legacy auction business model. So we've created the Computer Art Auction House - ACCA - in Web3 space as a revamp of the traditional form of auction.

The emergence of NFT technology introduced a range of tasks and issues that surfaced within the art market, such as:

  1. Identifying and acknowledging new computer art genres.
  2. Recognizing previously undiscovered artists and facilitating the entrance of fresh perspectives into established institutions.
  3. Identifying new curators who possess the potential to shape a renewed vision for the art world.
  4. Presenting an educational challenge for collectors and art enthusiasts to understand and navigate the realm of NFTs.
  5. Exploring technical and high-quality solutions to effectively showcase digital art in all its intricacies.
ACCA will focus of a community selection of fast-growing young digital artists and new generation of digital art experts. Auctions will be dedicated to showcasing exciting new genres of computer art, including glitch art, pixel art, generative art and artificial intelligence art. These groundbreaking art forms deserve recognition and celebration, and we're excited to introduce them to a broader audience through our auctions.

We're changing the approach to acquiring digital art by creating drops that combine the artworks of multiple artists in one auction drop with the smart contract revenue system.In upcoming articles, we will delve into comprehensive details regarding the semi-decentralized business model. This model encompasses a community-driven decision-making process and incorporates grants contests that aim to support the development of new computer art genres. These articles will also explore other related aspects and provide further insights.
